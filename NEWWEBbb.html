    About Cybersecurity

    Cybersecurity is an evolving field that focuses on protecting computer systems, networks, and data from cyber threats. As a cybersecurity professional, you'll learn to secure information and prevent unauthorized access to sensitive data.

    Our cybersecurity major provides hands-on training in threat detection, ethical hacking, cryptography, and more.
